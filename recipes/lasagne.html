Lasagne

Description

Why not treat the family to your own homemade Italian family dinner with an all-time favourite? Introducing our quick and easy lasagne recipe.

Ingrendients

  • 450g / 1lb of lean minced beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium green or red pepper
  • 1 x 400g / 14 oz. tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 dessertspoons of tomato sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 10 sheets of easi-cook lasagne sheets
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cheese sauce (see below)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C / 350°F / Gas Mark 4.
  2. Chop the onions, pepper, mushrooms and garlic.
  3. Take a look at our top tips on cooking with mince, then put the mince into a large pan and fry until completely brown, stirring all the time with a wooden spoon.
  4. Drain off any excess fat before adding onions and garlic to mince and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
  5. Add the chopped peppers, mushrooms, tin of tomatoes and tomato sauce to the mince mixture.
  6. Bring to the boil and leave to simmer on a low heat for 15 to 20 minutes.
  7. Put a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a casserole/lasagne dish, cover with easi-cook lasagne sheets.
  8. Make 1 or 2 more layers as above finishing with a layer of lasagne sheets. Cover the top layer with the cheese sauce.
  9. Sprinkle the top with grated cheese and bake for 30 to 40 minutes.
